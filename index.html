Flood Safety Tips

  • Move valuables and electrical appliances to higher ground before water rises.
  • Avoid walking or driving through moving flood water, even if it looks shallow.
  • Turn off electricity at the breaker if flooding is imminent in your home.
  • Keep a battery-powered radio and flashlight ready for power outages.
  • Follow evacuation orders from barangay officials without delay.
